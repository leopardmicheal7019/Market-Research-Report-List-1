<p><p>The 3D Digital Inspection market encompasses three main types: Machine Vision, Metrology, and Non-Destructive Testing (NDT). Machine Vision uses cameras and software to automate visual inspections for quality control, ensuring products meet specifications. Metrology focuses on precise measurement of physical dimensions to ensure accuracy and conformity in manufacturing processes. NDT is used to evaluate materials and components without causing damage, identifying defects or structural integrity. Together, these technologies enhance manufacturing efficiency, quality assurance, and safety across industries.</p></p>
