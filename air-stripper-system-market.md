<p><strong>The Air Stripper System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sieve Tray System</li><li>Packed Tower System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The air stripper system market includes two primary types: sieve tray systems and packed tower systems. Sieve tray systems utilize perforated trays to facilitate contact between air and liquid, promoting efficient mass transfer of volatile compounds. In contrast, packed tower systems employ packing materials to increase the surface area for gas-liquid interaction, enhancing the removal of contaminants. Both systems are designed for effective air stripping applications, commonly used in wastewater treatment and environmental remediation to eliminate volatile organic compounds.</p></p>
