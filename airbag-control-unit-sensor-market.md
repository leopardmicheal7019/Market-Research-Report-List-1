<p><strong>The Airbag Control Unit Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Mass Type Sensor</li><li>Roller Type Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Airbag Control Unit Sensor market includes Mass Type and Roller Type sensors, both crucial for vehicle safety. Mass Type sensors detect sudden deceleration by measuring the movement of a small mass within the sensor, triggering airbag deployment. Roller Type sensors use a rolling mechanism that shifts during impact, activating the airbag system. Both types ensure timely airbag deployment during collisions, enhancing passenger safety. Mass Type sensors are widely used for their reliability, while Roller Type sensors offer precision in detecting impact direction and severity.</p></p>
