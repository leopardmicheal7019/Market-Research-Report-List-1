<p><p>The Aircraft Towing Equipment market primarily comprises two types: towbar tractors and towbarless tractors. Towbar tractors are traditional vehicles equipped with a detachable towbar, allowing them to connect and maneuver aircraft safely on the ground. In contrast, towbarless tractors use a unique system to lift and push aircraft, eliminating the need for a towbar, leading to increased efficiency and versatility. Both types are essential for ground handling operations, enhancing aircraft movement and reducing turnaround times at airports.</p></p>
