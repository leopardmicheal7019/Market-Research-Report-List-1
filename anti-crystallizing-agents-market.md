<p><p>Anti-crystallizing agents are specialized additives used in various industries to inhibit or control the formation of crystalline structures in substances. The market is divided into two primary types: solid crystal anti-crystallizing agents and liquid solution anti-crystallizing agents. Solid crystal agents typically take the form of powders or granules, offering effective control during processes like freezing or drying. In contrast, liquid solution agents are often mixed with other solvents, providing uniform distribution and enhancing performance in applications such as food preservation and chemical manufacturing.</p></p>
