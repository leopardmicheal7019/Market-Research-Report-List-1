In contrast, liquid form flavors are more concentrated solutions that can enhance palatability and digestibility, often used in moist or liquid feed formulations. Both forms aim to improve animal feed attractiveness, ultimately enhancing feed intake and livestock performance, catering to diverse agricultural needs.</p></p>
