<p><strong>The Automobile Ultrasonic Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reflective Type</li><li>Transmission Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile ultrasonic sensor market comprises two main types: reflective type and transmission type sensors. Reflective type sensors emit ultrasonic waves that bounce off nearby objects, measuring distance based on the return time of the waves. These are commonly used for parking assistance. In contrast, transmission type sensors consist of paired transmitters and receivers, detecting obstacles by analyzing the interruption of ultrasonic waves between them. This type is often employed in collision avoidance systems, enhancing vehicle safety.</p></p>
