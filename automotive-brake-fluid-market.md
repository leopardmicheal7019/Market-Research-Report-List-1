<p><p>The automotive brake fluid market primarily comprises two types: mineral oil-based and synthetic brake fluids. Mineral oil brake fluids are derived from refined petroleum and offer adequate performance for standard vehicles but may degrade over time. In contrast, synthetic brake fluids are man-made and provide superior thermal stability, lower moisture absorption, and enhanced performance in extreme conditions. This distinction influences their application in various vehicle types, with synthetic fluids being preferred for high-performance and luxury vehicles due to their reliability and safety features.</p></p>
