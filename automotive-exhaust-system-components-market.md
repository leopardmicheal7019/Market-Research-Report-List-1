<p><p>The automotive exhaust system comprises several key components: the **Exhaust Manifold** collects gases from engine cylinders; the **Exhaust Pipe** channels these gases to the rear. The **Catalytic Converter** reduces harmful emissions by converting pollutants into less toxic substances. The **Exhaust Temperature Sensor** monitors heat levels to optimize performance. The **Car Muffler** minimizes noise by dampening sound waves. Finally, the **Exhaust Tail Pipe** releases treated gases into the atmosphere. Each component plays a vital role in ensuring efficient, eco-friendly, and quiet vehicle operation.</p></p>
