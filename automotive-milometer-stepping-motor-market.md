<p><ul><li>Permanent Magnet Type</li><li>Mixed Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive milometer stepping motor market consists of two main types: permanent magnet and mixed types. Permanent magnet stepping motors use permanent magnets for rotor positioning, allowing for precise movement and high torque at low speeds, making them suitable for odometer applications. Mixed type motors combine features of both permanent and reluctance motors, providing flexibility and improved performance in various conditions. These motors enhance reliability and accuracy in measuring vehicle mileage, contributing to their essential role in automotive instrumentation.</p></p>
