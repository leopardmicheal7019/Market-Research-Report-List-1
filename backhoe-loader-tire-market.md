<p><p>The backhoe loader tire market includes three main types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Solid tires are puncture-proof, durable, and ideal for harsh environments with debris or sharp objects. Polyurethane tires are lightweight, resistant to wear, and suitable for smooth surfaces, providing low rolling resistance and enhanced fuel efficiency.
