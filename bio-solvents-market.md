<p><p>The bio solvents market includes several types, each with distinct applications and characteristics. Bio-alcohols, like ethanol and butanol, serve as versatile solvents in coatings and adhesives. Bio-glycols and diols, such as propylene glycol, are used in pharmaceuticals and cosmetics. Lactate esters, derived from lactic acid, are known for their low toxicity and are used in personal care. D-limonene, from citrus peels, functions as a cleaner and degreaser, while methyl soyate, derived from soybeans, is popular in paint formulations and industrial applications.</p></p>
