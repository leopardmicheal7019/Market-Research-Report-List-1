Laminated type inductors utilize stacked sheets to minimize losses, ideal for high-frequency applications. Film type inductors use thin-film technology for precise inductance, suitable for compact devices. Weaving type inductors incorporate woven structures for flexibility and durability, while other types may include specialty designs tailored for unique requirements, addressing diverse industry demands.</p></p>
