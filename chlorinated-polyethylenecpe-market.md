<p><p>Chlorinated Polyethylene (CPE) is a versatile polymer derived from polyethylene, modified through chlorination to enhance its properties. The market is primarily divided into two types: Thermoplastic Resin Type and Elastomer Rubber Type. Thermoplastic CPE offers rigidity, heat resistance, and ease of processing, making it suitable for applications like coatings and wire insulation. In contrast, Elastomer Rubber CPE provides flexibility, resilience, and excellent weather resistance, making it ideal for seals, gaskets, and automotive components. Both types cater to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
