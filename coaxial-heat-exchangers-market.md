<p><p>Coaxial heat exchangers are specialized heat transfer devices that consist of two concentric pipes, allowing the transfer of heat between two fluids without mixing them. This design enhances efficiency by maximizing the surface area for heat exchange while minimizing the space required for installation. Their compact design makes them particularly advantageous in applications where space is limited, such as in HVAC systems, refrigeration, and industrial processes.</p><p>The Coaxial Heat Exchangers Market is experiencing significant growth, driven by increasing energy efficiency demands and the need for compact, high-performance heat transfer solutions across various industries.
