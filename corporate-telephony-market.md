<p><p>The corporate telephony market encompasses various systems for managing internal and external communications. An IP-PBX utilizes Voice over Internet Protocol to integrate voice and data, offering advanced features like call routing and voicemail over existing networks. Key Telephone Systems consist of several phones connected to a central unit, allowing direct line access for smaller businesses. Wireless Private Branch Exchange leverages wireless technology to enable mobile communications within an organization, providing flexibility and reducing cabling costs, ideal for dynamic work environments.</p></p>
