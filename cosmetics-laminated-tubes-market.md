<p><ul><li>ABL</li><li>PBL</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cosmetics laminated tubes market is primarily categorized into two types: ABL (Aluminium Barrier Laminates) and PBL (Plastic Barrier Laminates). ABL tubes feature a laminated structure with an aluminum layer that provides excellent barrier properties against air and moisture, ensuring product integrity and extended shelf life. In contrast, PBL tubes are made primarily of plastic and are known for their flexibility and lightweight nature, making them more suitable for intricate designs and vibrant printing options. Both types cater to diverse cosmetic packaging needs.</p></p>
