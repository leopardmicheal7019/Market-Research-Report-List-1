<p><strong>The Die Casting Machine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Chamber Die Casting Machine</li><li>Cold Chamber Die Casting Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The die casting machine market is primarily divided into two types: hot chamber and cold chamber die casting machines. Hot chamber machines are suitable for non-ferrous metals like zinc and magnesium, utilizing a gooseneck mechanism to feed molten metal directly into the die. In contrast, cold chamber machines are used for materials like aluminum and brass, where the metal is melted in a separate furnace and then transferred to the die for casting. Each type caters to specific manufacturing needs and material properties.</p></p>
