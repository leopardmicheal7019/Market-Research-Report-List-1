<p><ul><li>Bipolar</li><li>Unipolar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital Hall Effect sensors are categorized into bipolar and unipolar types based on their operational principles. Bipolar sensors activate with either pole of a magnet, detecting both north and south magnetic fields, allowing for versatile applications. In contrast, unipolar sensors respond only to one magnetic pole, typically the north pole, making them suitable for specific applications where directional sensing is needed. The choice between these types depends on the application's requirements for sensitivity, accuracy, and environmental factors.</p></p>
