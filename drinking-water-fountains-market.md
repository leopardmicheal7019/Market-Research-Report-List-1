<p><p>The drinking-water fountains market is primarily categorized into two types: Bulk Water Coolers (BWC) and Point of Use (POU) systems. BWCs serve high-capacity needs, offering large quantities of chilled water and are ideal for public spaces. On the other hand, POU systems are designed for individual or small-scale use, providing filtered water directly from a tap, ensuring convenience and sustainability. Both types focus on improving access to safe drinking water while catering to different consumer needs and environments.</p></p>
