<p><p>The electric loaders market is primarily divided into two types: full electric loaders and hybrid electric loaders. Full electric loaders operate entirely on electric power, offering zero emissions and reduced operational costs, making them ideal for eco-friendly applications. In contrast, hybrid electric loaders combine electric power with traditional internal combustion engines, providing flexibility and extended range while still improving fuel efficiency and reducing emissions. Both types contribute to the industry's shift towards sustainable and efficient construction machinery.</p></p>
