<p><p>The EVA waxes market is categorized into non-oxidized and oxidized types. Non-oxidized EVA waxes are characterized by their high molecular weight and excellent flexibility, making them ideal for applications in coatings, adhesives, and packaging. In contrast, oxidized EVA waxes possess increased polarity, offering enhanced adhesion and compatibility with polar materials, making them suitable for use in inks and industrial coatings. Both types of EVA waxes are crucial in various industries, providing unique properties that cater to specific applications.</p></p>
