<p><p>The fractional HP motor market includes two main types: fractional HP brushed motors and fractional HP brushless motors. Brushed motors use brushes to conduct electricity to the rotor, offering a simple design and lower cost, making them suitable for various applications. In contrast, brushless motors use electronic controllers for more efficient operation, resulting in higher performance, longer life, and reduced maintenance. The choice between these motor types depends on application requirements, cost considerations, and performance needs.</p></p>
