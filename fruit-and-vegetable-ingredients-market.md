<p><p>The fruit and vegetable ingredients market encompasses several key types. Concentrates involve extracting the juice or essence, offering intense flavors suited for various formulations. Pastes and purees provide a thick, smooth texture, ideal for soups and sauces. Pieces are whole or chopped fruits and vegetables, used for fresh applications or cooking. Powders represent dehydrated forms that enhance convenience and shelf life. NFC (Not From Concentrate) juices are freshly squeezed, retaining the original flavor and nutrients, appealing to health-conscious consumers.</p></p>
