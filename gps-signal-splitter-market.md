<p><ul><li>Active</li><li>Passive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The GPS signal splitter market is divided into two main types: active and passive splitters. Active splitters amplify the GPS signal, enabling transmission over longer distances and maintaining signal integrity, making them suitable for industrial applications. Conversely, passive splitters divide the signal without amplification, resulting in simpler designs and lower costs, commonly used in smaller installations. Both types serve distinct needs in satellite navigation systems, catering to varying requirements in terms of signal strength and complexity.</p></p>
