<p><p>Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) sensors are advanced geophysical tools used to penetrate the ground and provide detailed images of subsurface structures. They operate by emitting high-frequency electromagnetic waves into the ground, which are then reflected back to the surface, allowing for the detection of various materials, voids, and anomalies. GPR technology finds applications across numerous sectors, including civil engineering, archaeology, environmental studies, and utility detection.</p><p>The Ground Penetrating Radar (GPR) Sensor Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.3% during the forecast period.
