<p><p>The heavy bamboo market is categorized into cold pressing and hot pressing segments. Cold pressing involves processing bamboo at lower temperatures, maintaining its natural properties while producing environmentally-friendly products like fiber and panels. In contrast, hot pressing employs increased temperatures and pressure to enhance density and strength, yielding sturdier materials suitable for construction and furniture. Both methods cater to different needs, with cold pressing focusing on sustainability and aesthetics, while hot pressing emphasizes durability and structural integrity.</p></p>
