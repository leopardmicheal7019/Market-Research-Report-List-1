<p><p>The hydraulic loader crane market features two primary types: knuckle boom cranes and telescopic cranes. Knuckle boom cranes are characterized by their articulated arms that allow for greater maneuverability and versatility in tight spaces, making them ideal for urban construction and maintenance tasks. In contrast, telescopic cranes utilize extendable arms to reach greater heights and distances, making them suitable for heavy lifting in larger projects such as infrastructure development. Both types enhance efficiency and flexibility in material handling applications.</p></p>
