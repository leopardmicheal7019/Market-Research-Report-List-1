<p><ul><li>Straight Abutments</li><li>Angled Abutments</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The implant abutment market is primarily segmented into straight and angled abutments. Straight abutments are designed for optimal positioning in cases where the dental implant aligns directly with the prosthetic crown, providing a straightforward and stable attachment. In contrast, angled abutments are used to accommodate misalignments or anatomical constraints, allowing for the correct angulation of the prosthetic crown. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the aesthetic and functional outcomes of dental implant treatments.</p></p>
