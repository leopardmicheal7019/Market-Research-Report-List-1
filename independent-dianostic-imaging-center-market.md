<p><p>The Independent Diagnostic Imaging Center market is categorized into two primary types: the Heavy Asset Mode and the Light Asset Mode. The Heavy Asset Mode involves facilities that own and operate their imaging equipment, such as MRI and CT machines, leading to higher capital investment and operational expenses. In contrast, the Light Asset Mode, or Virtual Image Center, leverages partnerships with imaging equipment providers, allowing centers to offer diagnostic services without significant ownership costs, enhancing flexibility and reducing financial risk.</p></p>
