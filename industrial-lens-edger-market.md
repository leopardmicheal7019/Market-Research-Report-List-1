<p><p>The industrial lens edger market comprises three main types: manual, automatic, and semi-automatic lens edgers. Manual lens edgers require skilled operators to shape and polish lenses using hand tools, offering precise control but lower efficiency. Automatic lens edgers are fully automated, significantly enhancing production speed and consistency, ideal for high-volume operations. Semi-automatic lens edgers combine both manual and automated features, providing a balance between operator control and efficiency, making them versatile for various production needs.</p></p>
