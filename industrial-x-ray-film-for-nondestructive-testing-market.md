<p><p>Industrial X-ray film for nondestructive testing primarily consists of two types: non-screen type films and screen type films. Non-screen type films are sensitive to X-ray radiation directly, offering high resolution and detailed imaging, making them ideal for specific applications. In contrast, screen type films use fluorescent screens to amplify the X-ray exposure, resulting in faster processing times and reduced radiation doses. Each type serves unique needs in inspecting materials, welds, and components to ensure quality and safety in various industries.</p></p>
