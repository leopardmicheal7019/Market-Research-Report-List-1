<p><ul><li>Passive Infrared Fence</li><li>Active Infrared Fence</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The infrared fence market is divided into two main types: passive infrared fences and active infrared fences. Passive infrared fences utilize sensor technology to detect changes in heat emitted by individuals or animals, triggering an alarm when movement is detected. In contrast, active infrared fences employ a system of transmitter and receiver units to create an invisible barrier, alerting security personnel when the beam is disrupted. Both types are widely used in security applications for perimeters, enhancing safety and surveillance.</p></p>
