<p><p>The IV bags market comprises three main types: flex plastic IV bags, semi-rigid IV bags, and glass bottles. Flex plastic IV bags are lightweight and flexible, allowing easy handling and storage, making them popular for standard intravenous therapies. Semi-rigid IV bags offer enhanced stability, reducing the risk of breakage while maintaining some flexibility. Glass bottles, historically used, provide superior compatibility with various medications and protect against chemical interactions, though they are heavier and less convenient than plastic options.</p></p>
