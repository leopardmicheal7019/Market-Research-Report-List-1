<p><strong>The LED Chip Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Lateral Chip</li><li>Vertical Chip</li><li>Flip Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The LED chip market comprises three main types: lateral chips, vertical chips, and flip chips. Lateral chips, often used in low-power applications, have conductive layers on the same side as the light-emitting surface. Vertical chips, designed for high-power applications, feature a vertical structure for enhanced thermal management and efficiency. Flip chips utilize an inverted configuration, improving thermal conductivity and electrical performance, making them suitable for high-density applications. Each type serves distinct applications based on performance and efficiency needs.</p></p>
