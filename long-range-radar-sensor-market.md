<p><ul><li>Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensor</li><li>Long Range Laser Radar Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Long Range Radar Sensor market includes two primary types: Long Range Millimeter Wave Radar Sensors and Long Range Laser Radar Sensors. Millimeter wave radar sensors operate in the frequency range of 30 to 300 GHz, providing high-resolution object detection and tracking in various environmental conditions. In contrast, laser radar sensors, or LiDAR, use pulsed laser light to measure distances, offering precise imaging and superior resolution for 3D mapping and navigation. Both technologies enhance safety and automation in automotive and aerospace applications.</p></p>
