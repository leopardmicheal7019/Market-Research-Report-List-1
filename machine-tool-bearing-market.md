<p><ul><li>BallBearing</li><li>RollerBearing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The machine tool bearing market is primarily divided into two main types: ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings utilize spherical balls to reduce friction between moving parts, offering high speed and precision, making them ideal for lightweight applications. Roller bearings, on the other hand, employ cylindrical rollers, providing greater load-carrying capacity and stability, suited for heavy-duty applications. Both types are essential in enhancing the performance, reliability, and efficiency of machine tools across various industrial sectors.</p></p>
