<p><p>The metalworking gas scrubbers market comprises different types that efficiently manage emissions. Burn scrubbers use combustion to treat gases, while plasma scrubbers utilize high-energy plasma to break down pollutants. Heat wet scrubbers cool exhaust gases in a liquid medium, effectively removing contaminants. Dry scrubbers absorb or react with harmful substances without water, offering a space-efficient solution. Each type serves specific applications, enhancing air quality and ensuring compliance with environmental regulations in metalworking processes.</p></p>
