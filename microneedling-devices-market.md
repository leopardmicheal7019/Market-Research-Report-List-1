<p><p>Microneedling devices play a crucial role in skin rejuvenation and therapy, categorized into three primary types: Derma-Stamps, Dermapens, and Dermarollers. Derma-Stamps employ a flat needle configuration for precision in targeted areas, ideal for enhancing scars and fine lines. Dermapens utilize a motorized mechanism for adjustable needle depth, allowing for greater control and efficiency in treatment. Dermarollers, featuring a rolling mechanism with numerous needles, are popular for at-home use, providing a cost-effective option for skin treatment and rejuvenation.</p></p>
