<p><strong>The Optical Beam Profiler Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Camera Based</li><li>Scanning Split</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical beam profiler market is primarily divided into two types: camera-based profilers and scanning split profilers. Camera-based profilers utilize high-resolution cameras to capture beam profiles, offering real-time analysis and ease of use for various applications. On the other hand, scanning split profilers employ a mechanical mechanism to analyze the beam by splitting and scanning it across designated areas, delivering precise measurements of beam characteristics. Both types serve critical roles in optimizing laser performance across diverse industries.</p></p>
