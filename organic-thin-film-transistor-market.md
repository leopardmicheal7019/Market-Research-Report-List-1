<p><p>The Organic Thin Film Transistor (OTFT) market includes two key types: Organic Field-Effect Transistors (OFETs) and Organic Electrochemical Transistors (OECTs). OFETs operate by modulating electrical current through an organic semiconductor layer, making them ideal for flexible electronics like displays and sensors. OECTs, on the other hand, use ion migration in an electrolyte to control current, excelling in bioelectronics and wearable devices due to their sensitivity to biological signals. Both types leverage organic materials for lightweight, flexible, and cost-effective solutions.</p></p>
