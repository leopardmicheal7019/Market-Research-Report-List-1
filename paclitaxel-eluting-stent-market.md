<p><p>Paclitaxel-eluting stents are specialized medical devices used in the treatment of coronary artery disease. These stents are coated with paclitaxel, a chemotherapy drug that inhibits cell proliferation and reduces the risk of restenosis, or the re-narrowing of blood vessels after angioplasty. By releasing paclitaxel slowly into the surrounding tissue, these stents promote healing and minimize complications associated with traditional metal stents.</p><p>The Paclitaxel-eluting Stent Market is experiencing significant growth due to the rising prevalence of cardiovascular diseases, advancements in stent technology, and an increasing aging population. Factors contributing to market expansion include the growing awareness of minimally invasive procedures, favorable regulatory frameworks, and continuous improvements in healthcare infrastructure.</p><p>Latest trends in this market involve the development of next-generation drug-eluting stents that enhance patient outcomes while reducing complications.
