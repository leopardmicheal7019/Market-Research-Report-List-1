<p><p>The Passenger Vehicle Keyless Go market includes two main types: Passive Keyless Entry System (PKES) and Remote Keyless Entry System (RKES). PKES allows drivers to unlock and start their vehicle without physically using a key, relying on proximity sensors and automatic authentication. RKES requires manual interaction, such as pressing a button on a key fob to lock, unlock, or start the vehicle. Both systems enhance convenience and security, with PKES offering a more seamless, hands-free experience compared to RKES.</p></p>
