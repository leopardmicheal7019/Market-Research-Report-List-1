<p><p>The PE blister packaging market is categorized primarily into two types: thermoforming and cold forming. Thermoforming involves heating plastic sheets until pliable, then molding them into desired shapes to create robust, transparent packages, ideal for consumer products. Cold forming, on the other hand, utilizes aluminum foil and plastic without heat, ensuring excellent barrier properties and protection for sensitive items, such as pharmaceuticals. Together, these methods cater to diverse industries, enhancing product visibility and extending shelf life.</p></p>
