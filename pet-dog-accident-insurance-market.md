<p><p>Pet Dog Accident Insurance is categorized into two main types: Lifetime Cover and Non-lifetime Cover. Lifetime Cover provides coverage for ongoing conditions throughout a dog's life, ensuring that costs related to chronic injuries or illnesses are covered as long as premiums are paid. In contrast, Non-lifetime Cover offers protection for specific incidents or injuries but limits coverage for pre-existing conditions after the policy period ends. These distinctions help pet owners choose insurance that aligns with their dog's health needs and their financial preferences.</p></p>
