<p><p>The polyester artificial vascular market can be segmented into low porosity and high porosity types. Low porosity vascular grafts offer minimal permeability, enhancing strength and durability, making them suitable for high-pressure environments. In contrast, high porosity grafts are designed with increased permeability, facilitating better integration with surrounding tissues and promoting faster healing. The choice between these types depends on specific clinical requirements, patient conditions, and the desired balance between structural integrity and biological compatibility in vascular applications.</p></p>
