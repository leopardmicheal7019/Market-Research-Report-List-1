<p><ul><li>FPD X-Ray Inspection System</li><li>Film X-Ray Inspection System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Portable X-Ray Inspection System market consists of two main types: FPD (Flat Panel Detector) X-Ray Inspection Systems and Film X-Ray Inspection Systems. FPD X-Ray systems utilize digital flat panel detectors to capture high-quality images quickly, offering advantages like enhanced image processing and real-time analysis. In contrast, Film X-Ray Inspection Systems rely on traditional film for image capture, providing a cost-effective solution, albeit with longer processing times. Both types serve industries such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing for non-destructive testing and quality assurance.</p></p>
