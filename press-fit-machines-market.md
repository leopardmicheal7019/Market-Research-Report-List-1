<p><ul><li>Manual Press Fit Machines</li><li>Semi-automatic Press Fit Machines</li><li>Fully-automatic Press Fit Machines</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Press fit machines are categorized into three types based on their operational capabilities. Manual press fit machines require operator involvement for alignment and force application, ideal for low-volume production or maintenance tasks. Semi-automatic press fit machines automate certain functions, such as feeding and positioning components, balancing efficiency and operator control for medium-volume applications. Fully automatic press fit machines integrate advanced controls for complete automation, enhancing accuracy and speed, making them suitable for high-volume manufacturing processes.</p></p>
