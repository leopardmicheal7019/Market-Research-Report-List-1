<p><p>The radiographic systems market is categorized into three main types: analog, digital, and others. Analog systems utilize traditional film-based methods for capturing images, relying on chemical processing. Digital systems represent a modern approach, using electronic sensors to convert images into digital form, enabling easier storage, manipulation, and sharing. The "others" category encompasses advanced technologies like computed radiography, which bridges analog and digital, and emerging innovations such as portable mobile imaging devices, enhancing diagnostic capabilities in diverse settings.</p></p>
