Solid tires, made of rubber or composite materials, are puncture-proof and durable, suited for heavy-duty or industrial RVs. Polyurethane tires, lightweight and resistant to wear, provide excellent load-bearing capacity and longevity, often used in compact or specialized RVs. Each type caters to specific needs, balancing performance, durability, and application requirements.</p></p>
