<p><p>The Resistance Welding Service market encompasses various welding techniques, each serving specific applications. Spot welding involves joining two metal surfaces at localized points, ideal for sheet metal assembly. Projection welding creates welds at specified projections on metal parts, enhancing strength. Seam welding produces continuous welds along edges, commonly used in containers. Butt welding effectively connects two metal pieces end-to-end, while resistance bolt welding joins fasteners to metal substrates, ensuring durability. Each method is crucial for manufacturing, automotive, and construction industries.</p></p>
