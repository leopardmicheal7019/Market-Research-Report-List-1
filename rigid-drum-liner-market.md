<p><p>The rigid drum liner market comprises various types based on materials, including PE (Polyethylene), PP (Polypropylene), PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride), and PVDC (Polyvinylidene Chloride) liners. PE drum liners are lightweight and resistant to chemicals, ideal for food and beverages. PP liners offer strength and durability, suitable for industrial applications. PVC liners provide good flexibility and barrier properties, making them versatile for various contents. PVDC liners boast excellent barrier properties against moisture and gases, enhancing product shelf life, particularly in food packaging.</p></p>
