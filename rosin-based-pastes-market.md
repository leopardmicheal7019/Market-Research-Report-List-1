<p><p>Rosin-based pastes are classified into three market types based on their activity levels. Low activity level pastes are suitable for applications requiring minimal fluxing action, typically used in non-critical soldering processes. Moderate activity level pastes offer a balanced performance, making them versatile for a variety of assemblies. High activity level pastes contain stronger fluxing agents, effectively removing oxidation and contaminants, making them ideal for challenging surfaces and more demanding soldering applications. Each type caters to specific production needs.</p></p>
