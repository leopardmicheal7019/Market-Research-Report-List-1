<p><p>The semi-automatic blast cabinet market features two main types: pressure blast cabinets and suction blast cabinets. Pressure blast cabinets utilize compressed air to propel abrasive materials at high speeds, allowing for efficient and powerful cleaning or surface preparation. In contrast, suction blast cabinets rely on a vacuum system to draw abrasives, providing a gentler approach ideal for delicate surfaces. Both types enhance productivity and precision in various industrial applications, catering to diverse needs in finishing and surface treatment processes.</p></p>
