<p><p>The semiconductor chemical-mechanical polishing (CMP) material market primarily comprises CMP pads and CMP slurries. CMP pads are abrasive surfaces used to apply pressure and facilitate the polishing process on semiconductor wafers, ensuring a smooth finish. CMP slurries, on the other hand, are mixtures containing chemicals and abrasive particles that assist in the chemical and mechanical removal of material during polishing. Together, these components are essential for enhancing wafer surface quality and achieving the precision required in semiconductor manufacturing.</p></p>
