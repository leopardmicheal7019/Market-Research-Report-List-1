<p><p>The semiconductor controlled rectifier (SCR) market is divided into unidirectional and bidirectional SCR modules. Unidirectional SCR modules allow current to flow in one direction, primarily used in applications like power supplies and motor controls. In contrast, bidirectional SCR modules enable current flow in both directions, making them suitable for alternating current applications such as AC voltage control and lighting systems. Both types enhance efficiency and control in various industrial, commercial, and residential applications, driving their market demand.</p></p>
