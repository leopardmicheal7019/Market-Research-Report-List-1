<p><p>The signal transmission wire and cable market encompasses three primary types: coaxial, fiber optic, and twisted pair cables. Coaxial cables are widely used for video and data transmission due to their durability and shielding against electromagnetic interference. Fiber optic cables offer high-speed data transmission over long distances with minimal signal loss, making them ideal for internet and telecommunication applications. Twisted pair cables, commonly used in networking, are cost-effective and designed to reduce crosstalk by twisting pairs of wires together. </p></p>
