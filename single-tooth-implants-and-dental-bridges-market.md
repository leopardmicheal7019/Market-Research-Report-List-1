<p><p>Single tooth implants and dental bridges are popular restorative dental procedures used to replace missing teeth. A single tooth implant involves surgically placing a titanium post into the jawbone, which acts as a root for a crown that mimics the appearance and function of a natural tooth. Conversely, dental bridges consist of crowns that are placed on adjacent teeth to span the gap left by a missing tooth, providing both aesthetic and functional restoration.</p><p>The market for single tooth implants and dental bridges is experiencing significant growth, driven by factors such as increasing awareness of dental health, advancements in implant technology, and a rising aging population that requires dental solutions.
