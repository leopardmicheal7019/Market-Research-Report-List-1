<p><p>The single-use bioreactors (SUBs) market is segmented into wave-induced motion, stirred, and others. Wave-induced motion SUBs utilize gentle shaking to create fluid movement, promoting cell growth while minimizing shear stress. Stirred SUBs employ mechanical agitation for mixing, enabling robust cultures at larger scales. Other types may include hybrid systems or specialized designs for unique applications. Each type caters to varying bioprocess demands, offering flexibility, reduced cleaning requirements, and faster turnaround in biomanufacturing.</p></p>
