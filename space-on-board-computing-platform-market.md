<p><strong>The Space On-board Computing Platform Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Low Earth Orbit (LEO)</li><li>Medium Earth Orbit (MEO)</li><li>Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Space On-board Computing Platform market is categorized into three primary orbit types. Low Earth Orbit (LEO) platforms are used for applications such as Earth observation and satellite communication, providing lower latency. Medium Earth Orbit (MEO) is primarily utilized for navigation and tracking systems, offering a balance of coverage and signal quality. Geostationary Earth Orbit (GEO) platforms are essential for telecommunications and broadcasting, maintaining a fixed position relative to the Earth, which allows for continuous coverage of specific areas.</p></p>
