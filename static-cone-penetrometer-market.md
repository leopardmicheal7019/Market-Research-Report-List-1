<p><p>The static cone penetrometer market is characterized by two primary types: hand-operated and self-start models. Hand-operated static cone penetrometers rely on manual force to drive the cone into the soil, offering portability and simplicity for small-scale projects. In contrast, self-start static cone penetrometers are mechanized, allowing for consistent penetration and reduced physical effort, making them suitable for larger or more complex sites. Both types are essential for soil testing and geotechnical assessments, providing valuable data on soil properties.</p></p>
