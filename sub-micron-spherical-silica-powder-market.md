<p><p>The sub-micron spherical silica powder market includes two primary types: nanometer grade and 0.1 micron particles. Nanometer grade silica features ultra-fine particles typically less than 100 nanometers, offering superior surface area and reactivity, ideal for advanced applications in electronics, cosmetics, and pharmaceuticals. In contrast, 0.1 micron silica particles, slightly larger but still sub-micron, provide a balance of performance and cost-effectiveness, commonly used in coatings, fillers, and various industrial applications. Both types enhance product performance in their respective fields.</p></p>
