<p><p>The temperature and humidity sensors ICs market encompasses various technologies catering to different applications. Thermostats and thermocouples measure temperature, while PT100 sensors utilize a platinum resistance element for precise readings. Infrared temperature sensors detect non-contact temperatures. Humidity sensors include capacitive types, which measure changes in capacitance, resistive sensors that gauge resistance variations, and thermal conductor sensors that determine humidity by analyzing thermal conductivity changes. Each type offers unique advantages suitable for diverse industrial, commercial, and consumer applications.</p></p>
