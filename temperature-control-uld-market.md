<p><p>The Temperature Control Unit Load Device (ULD) market is categorized into two main types: active ULD and passive ULD. Active ULDs are equipped with built-in temperature control systems, using power sources to maintain specific temperature ranges for sensitive cargo, like pharmaceuticals and perishables. In contrast, passive ULDs rely on insulation and thermal liners to maintain temperatures, without active cooling or heating mechanisms. Both types are crucial for ensuring the safe and efficient transport of temperature-sensitive goods in the air freight industry.</p></p>
