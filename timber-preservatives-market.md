<p><strong>The Timber Preservatives Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Water Based</li><li>Oil Based</li><li>Solvent Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The timber preservatives market can be categorized into three main types: water-based, oil-based, and solvent-based preservatives. Water-based preservatives are popular for their low odor and quick drying properties, making them environmentally friendly and easy to clean. Oil-based preservatives penetrate deeply into the wood, providing long-lasting protection against moisture and pests. Solvent-based preservatives combine the benefits of both water and oil, often offering enhanced longevity but may emit stronger odors. Each type serves different application needs and preferences.</p></p>
