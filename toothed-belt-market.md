<p><strong>The Toothed Belt Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>One Side</li><li>Double Sided</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toothed belt market is categorized into two main types: one-sided and double-sided belts. One-sided toothed belts feature teeth on only one side, ideal for applications where power is transmitted in a single direction. In contrast, double-sided toothed belts have teeth on both sides, allowing for flexible operation and power transmission in either direction. Each type serves specific mechanical needs, with one-sided belts often used in straightforward drives, while double-sided options are preferred in more complex systems requiring versatility.</p></p>
