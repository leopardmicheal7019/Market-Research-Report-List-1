<p><p>The truck exhaust system market includes two main types: single and dual exhaust systems. A single exhaust system uses one pipe to expel gases, offering simplicity, cost-effectiveness, and space efficiency, making it common in standard trucks. A dual exhaust system features two pipes, enhancing engine performance, fuel efficiency, and sound quality, often preferred for high-performance or heavy-duty trucks. Both systems aim to reduce emissions and improve engine efficiency, with the choice depending on vehicle requirements and performance goals.</p></p>
