<p><ul><li>Solid</li><li>Hollow</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The truck stabilizer market includes two main types: solid and hollow stabilizers. Solid stabilizers are made from a single piece of durable material, offering high strength and rigidity, ideal for heavy-duty applications. Hollow stabilizers, on the other hand, are lightweight due to their hollow core, reducing vehicle weight and improving fuel efficiency while maintaining sufficient strength for most applications. Both types enhance vehicle stability and handling, with the choice depending on load requirements and performance priorities.</p></p>
