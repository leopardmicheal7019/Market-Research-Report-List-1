<p><strong>The UV Cureable Resin Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solventborne UV Resins</li><li>Waterborne UV Resins</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The UV curable resin market comprises two main types: solventborne UV resins and waterborne UV resins. Solventborne UV resins utilize organic solvents, offering high performance and quick curing, making them suitable for various industrial applications. However, they may pose environmental and health concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, waterborne UV resins use water as a solvent, providing a more eco-friendly option with lower VOC emissions, appealing to manufacturers seeking sustainable solutions while still achieving efficient curing and robust performance.</p></p>
