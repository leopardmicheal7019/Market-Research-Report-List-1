<p><p>The vegetative roof system market comprises three main types: extensive, semi-intensive, and intensive. Extensive roofs feature shallow substrate, supporting drought-resistant plants, making them low-maintenance and lightweight. Semi-intensive roofs offer medium substrate depth, accommodating a wider variety of plants, requiring moderate maintenance. Intensive roofs have deeper substrates, allowing for diverse plantings, including trees and shrubs, but necessitating more maintenance and structural support. Each type serves different environmental goals, aesthetic desires, and structural capabilities, catering to various urban greening needs.</p></p>
