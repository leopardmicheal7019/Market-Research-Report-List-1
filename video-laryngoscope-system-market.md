<p><strong>The Video Laryngoscope System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Un-Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li><li>Channelled Video Laryngoscopes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The video laryngoscope system market comprises two main types: un-channelled and channelled video laryngoscopes. Un-channelled video laryngoscopes offer a direct view of the airway without any guiding channel, making them suitable for straightforward intubations. In contrast, channelled video laryngoscopes feature a built-in guide for the endotracheal tube, facilitating more complex intubation procedures, especially in difficult airway scenarios. Both types enhance visualization and improve the success rates of intubations in clinical settings.</p></p>
