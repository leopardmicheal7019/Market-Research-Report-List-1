<p><p>Water-based spray adhesives encompass various types including epoxy, polyurethane, synthetic rubber, and vinyl acetate ethylene. Epoxy adhesives provide strong bonding and durability, suitable for industrial applications. Polyurethane offers flexibility and moisture resistance, ideal for woodworking and upholstery. Synthetic rubber adhesives are versatile, catering to a wide range of surfaces with quick tack properties. Vinyl acetate ethylene adhesives are known for their high strength and low environmental impact, commonly used in packaging and crafts. Each type serves unique applications based on performance and requirements.</p></p>
