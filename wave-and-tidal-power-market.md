<p><p>Wave and tidal power markets focus on harnessing energy from oceanic movements. Wave power utilizes the energy generated by surface waves, capturing it through various technologies like point absorbers and oscillating water columns. In contrast, tidal power relies on the gravitational pull of the moon and sun, converting the kinetic and potential energy of tidal movements into electricity, often using barrages or underwater turbines. Both markets offer renewable energy solutions, contributing to sustainable development and reducing reliance on fossil fuels.</p></p>
