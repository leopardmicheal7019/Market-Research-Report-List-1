<p><p>The windscreen wipers market is primarily divided into two types: boneless wipers and bone wipers. Boneless wipers feature a sleek design without a traditional frame, offering improved aerodynamics and reduced noise during operation. They provide consistent, even pressure on the windscreen for effective cleaning. In contrast, bone wipers utilize a more traditional structure with a framework that supports the wiper blade, often favored for durability and performance in harsh weather conditions. Both types aim to enhance visibility and driving safety.</p></p>
